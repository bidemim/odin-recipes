Fried Plantain

Description

Fried plantain is a staple in many homes all over the world. From the Carribeans to the coasts of Africa, fried plantain is a side dish that lends sweetness to all dishes it accompanies.

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil.
  • 3 ripe plantains.

Steps

  1. Peel three ripe plantains and cut either diagonally or into cubes.
  2. Add some oil to a frying pan and heat at medium low for about two minutes.
  3. Add some pieces of the plantain to the pan.
  4. Fry until golden and turn the other side.
  5. Turn down the heat once both sides are golden brown and take out the plantains.
  6. Add the next batch to the oil and keep frying until you're all done.
  7. Serve when cool with a side of rice, beans, soup or some stew.
