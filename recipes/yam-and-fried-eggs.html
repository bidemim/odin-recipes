Yam and Fried Eggs

Description

Yam and Fried Egg sauce is a staple in many homes in West Africa. It is commonly had for breakfast and is rich in carbs, protein with lots of vitamins. This recipe makes about 3 servings of the meal.

Ingredients

  • 1kg African yam..
  • 5 eggs.
  • 4 large tomatoes.
  • 1 medium sized onion.
  • Half cup cooking oil.
  • 1 teaspoon each of salt and seasoning.
  • 2 large bell or sweet peppers.
  • Optional2 habanero, scotch bonnet (or some other hot pepper).

Steps

  1. Peel the yam and cut into small pieces.
  2. Wash the pieces in cold water.
  3. Put the pieces in a pot and add enough water to cover all pieces.
  4. Set your stove to medium high and let it boil.
  5. While the yam is boiling, chop the peppers and onion in a food processor.
  6. Add half a cup of oil to a pan and fry the pepper mix for about 3 minutes.
  7. Add about a teaspoon of salt, and a teaspoon of seasoning to the mix.
  8. In a seperate bowl, mix the eggs until smooth.
  9. Add the eggs to the pepper mix and turn down the heat to low.
  10. Mix the egss with the pepper and turn the heat to medium.
  11. Let it simmer for about 5 more minutes.
  12. Mix once more and let it cook for two more minutes. Then turn off the stove.
  13. If a fork passes through a yam piece cleanly, turn off the stove and gently drain the excess water.
  14. Serve once cool. Bon Apetit!
